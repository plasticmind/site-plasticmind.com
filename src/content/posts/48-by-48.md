<pre><code>        &lt;p&gt;One of my good friends &lt;a href="https://www.drewbe.com/"&gt;Drew Johnson&lt;/a&gt; just started something he&amp;#8217;s calling &lt;a href="https://48by48.com/"&gt;48 by 48&lt;/a&gt;.  He&amp;#8217;s going to be traveling to each of the 48 continental states&amp;#8212;one a week&amp;#8212;and will be volunteering in every state along the way. As he does, he&amp;#8217;s going to be videoing, blogging and twittering the whole journey.&lt;/p&gt;

        &lt;!--more--&gt;&lt;p&gt;He&amp;#8217;s currently looking for three important things:&lt;/p&gt;
</code></pre>

**1. Needs:** Drew needs places in every state to volunteer, everything from homeless shelters to building houses to non-profits that need websites. He&#8217;ll consider any needs that are presented so long as they are genuinely geared towards helping those in need.

**2. Resources:** Drew needs places to stay, money for gas and food. He&#8217;s even going to try to raise awareness for one particular project a week from the place where he&#8217;s serving and raise money for that particular project.

**3. Promotion:** Drew needs people to talk about the project. He&#8217;s still working on getting out more information like an introductory video, but he&#8217;s got a lot of places where you can go to keep up with the project: <a href="https://facebook.com/48by48/">Facebook fan page</a>, <a href="https://twitter.com/48by48/">Twitter account</a> and <a href="https://www.48by48.com/blog">blog</a>. 

The most important thing is that this is about more than just Drew traveling the country: he&#8217;s trying to promote the STO (service-to-others) lifestyle and encouraging others to get on board and participate.

Even if you can&#8217;t <a href="https://www.48by48.com/support/">contribute</a> directly to him, maybe you could help Drew by spreading the word?
