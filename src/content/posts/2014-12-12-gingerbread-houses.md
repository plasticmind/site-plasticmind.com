One of our favorite traditions of the Christmas season is making gingerbread houses with the children.  Thankfully, they're at the age where their creativity is starting to take shape and it isn't just Mom and Dad building the houses.

Though we did have to help them frame the house.

<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5151" src="/assets/i/gb-1.jpg" alt="gb-1" width="1024" height="682" />

Thankfully, this year's frosting held better than in previous years.

<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5152" src="/assets/i/gb-2.jpg" alt="gb-2" width="1024" height="682" />

Oh look, a camera...

<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5153" src="/assets/i/gb-3.jpg" alt="gb-3" width="1024" height="682" /><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5154" src="/assets/i/gb-4.jpg" alt="gb-4" width="1024" height="682" />

Once the houses were assembled, two important questions remained: Is this house strong enough to hold all the candy I intend to cover it with?

<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5156" src="/assets/i/gb-6.jpg" alt="gb-6" width="1024" height="682" />

And which candy should I choose...

<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5159" src="/assets/i/gb-9.jpg" alt="gb-9" width="1024" height="682" />

The candy construction begins! No candy coin unwrapped! No sprinkle un-sprinkled! No Swedish fish left behind!

<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5157" src="/assets/i/gb-7.jpg" alt="gb-7" width="1024" height="682" /><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5161" src="/assets/i/gb-11.jpg" alt="gb-11" width="1024" height="682" />

All the while, Elijah looked on with ferocity.  I think it was less "How much candy are they going to put on those things?!" and more "If only I could crawl, the things I would do to those houses..."

<a href="/assets/i/gb-5.jpg"><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5155" src="/assets/i/gb-5.jpg" alt="gb-5" width="1024" height="682" /></a>

An obligatory kitschy Christmas tree shot.

<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5160" src="/assets/i/gb-10.jpg" alt="gb-10" width="1024" height="682" />

Ethan thought it would be a better idea to apply the frosting to the candy instead of the house.  Live and learn, son.

<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5166" src="/assets/i/gb-16.jpg" alt="gb-16" width="1024" height="682" />

Putting the finishing touches on the houses.  Anna obsessed over every detail, being sure to arrange her candy by color.

<a href="/assets/i/gb-11.jpg"><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5163" src="/assets/i/gb-13.jpg" alt="gb-13" width="1024" height="682" /><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5162" src="/assets/i/gb-12.jpg" alt="gb-12" width="1024" height="682" /></a>
