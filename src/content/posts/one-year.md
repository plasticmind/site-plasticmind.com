Hard to believe, but it was exactly one year ago today that I proposed to <a href="https://plasticmind.com/photos/archives/my_lady/index.html">Jessica</a>, and in just three weeks we'll be getting married.  I can still remember tossing and turning in that little camper outside the Guay's house, running through my head over and over <a href="https://plasticmind.com/theboyandthegirl-page1.html">just exactly what I'd say to her</a>, how I'd gesture, imagining her reaction.  I guess time does make itself wings...
