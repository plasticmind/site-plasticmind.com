Attention all Movable Typers... Thanks to Jesse from Simultaneity, I've found the must have program of the month!  MTClient is a free download from XREware that lets you post to your blog without touching a browser icon.  With built in spell check and the ability to save your posts to your hard drive, this is one great find.  It even supports multiple blogs, multiple categories and all the keywords you can muster.  If you don't have any MT client, definitely check this one out.  It's worth the download.
