Because we must face truth.  We must procure a reason for our fear, for our weakness.  He does not settle for lies or convenient phrases, but for TRUTH - truth so pure and so stark that self in never comfortable.  And the real truth is that we are utterly without hope.  So our hatred is abated because while His demand for truth burns like fire, His offer of grace gives us our only hope.<br />
