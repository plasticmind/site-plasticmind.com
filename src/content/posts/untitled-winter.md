The touch of wool, the rasping scrape<br />
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Across the neck and chin,<br />
The taste of snowflakes on the tongue,<br />
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The <font color="red">ruby-ripened</font> skin,<br />
The crayoned nose, frost-bitten tip,<br />
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The patches where the feeble slip,<br />
The children laugh, the father's play<br />
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And seize the life from every day.
