*A poem concieved upon reading the newspaper and looking around in church:*





Warm me sun and bathe me brine<br />
Here where rolls the gentle tide,<br />
Strokes the ocean bottom fine.





Close my eyes, suspended low<br />
Between the sky and ribboned floor,<br />
Anchored, spinning by a toe.





Yes, I saw the coming storm;<br />
I happened once to look around--<br />
The great tsunami taking form.<br />
But I cannot hear a sound.





Sun feels good on eyelids closed.<br />
Flow feels good on muscles loose.<br />
Do not speak of things to come<br />
In the shallow ocean water.
