And finally, in honor of birthdays, I'm going naked tomorrow.  That's right, I'm taking it all off.  It just so happens that tomorrow is the <a href="https://www.dustindiaz.com/naked-day/">First Annual Naked Day</a> when everyone sheds their stylesheets and the world gets to see pure, unstyled site content.  A birthday suit day on my birthday... how appropriate!

It's not easy "baring all".  So in light of a tired style and ugly code, I am making a vow.  I hereby vow to keep my site naked until I have the new and improved PlasticMind Blog up and running.  (Hint, it involves trashing my photoblog)  Good incentive for a web designer.  Look for it soon...
