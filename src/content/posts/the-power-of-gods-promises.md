You can listen to a copy of the message I preached in church Sunday morning.  The sermon is from Isaiah 40 and discusses the value that God's promises hold because of the greatness of God.





The absolutely hilarious part about this (and you'll get it after listening to the first 2 minutes) is that this thing is about 37 and a half minutes.  Why is that funny?  When I used to teach school, the periods were 38 minutes long; now I've got this Pavlovian habit to speak publically in 38 minute chunks.  Ha.
