I'm working hard to build a new business, but several folks in the space are miles ahead of me and crushing it. At first, that got me feeling discouraged, but I reminded myself (saying it out loud in case anyone else needs to hear it): You have valuable skills and experience. Stop comparing. Focus on your strengths and get moving!
