Iron, angry sky<br />
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Frantic, fuming thunderhead<br />
Far flung drops of night.





Crystal in the air<br />
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Sweeps across vanilla fields<br />
To join the others.
