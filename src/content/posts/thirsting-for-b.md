Sunlight fades, and now decends the unnamed fear that haunts both mind and body with a horrible horror-untamed, unaccepted, believed and all essentially true.<br />
 <br />
But beauty bursts upon the scene and takes away breath, stops a heart and opens tired eyes.<br />
 <br />
Somewhere a stream is flowing and covering a dry, parched soul; and most surely that dry, parched soul is my own, and the sweet refreshing flow is beauty.<br />
