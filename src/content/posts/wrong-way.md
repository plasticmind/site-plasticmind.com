I woke up early this morning and got the crazy notion to visit an abandon part of a nearby town with my camera.  See, there's this dead end road with piles and piles of abandon you-name-it.  Then, I found an old Route 13 off-ramp (<a href="https://maps.google.com/maps?f=q&hl=en&q=bucks+county+courier+times&sll=40.175316,-74.872004&sspn=0.010477,0.014377&layer=&ie=UTF8&z=18&ll=40.145646,-74.812791&spn=0.00262,0.003594&t=k&om=1">right across from the Bucks County Courier Times building</a>) that's been blocked off for years and is all grown up.  There's even an old dilapidated billboard covered in graffiti.  I only had to pull twelve ticks out of my flesh afterwards!  Check out the result of my adventures in <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/plasticmind/sets/72157600115984552/">my "Wrong Way?" Flickr photoset</a>.
