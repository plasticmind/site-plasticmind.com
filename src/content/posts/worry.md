Worry is a hamster wheel.<br />
 <br />
Round and round and round it goes, squeaking all the while, as we leap breathlessly to the next rung, accomplishing so much of nothing.<br />
 <br />
Like a drug, it penetrates our mind and soon the molehills are mountains, the sky is the sea, and drops of rain are transformed from tears of joy to tears of pain.<br />
